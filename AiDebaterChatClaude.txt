Component 1 — Argument Parser
What it does:
Takes your raw text input and breaks it into structured components:
Main Claim  — what you're ultimately asserting
Premises  — the reasons you give to support it
Implicit Assumptions  — things you're taking for granted without saying

Component 2 — Weakness Scorer
What it does:
Takes each premise and assumption and scores how logically vulnerable it is on 3 dimensions:
Evidence Scor e — is this claim supported by data or just assertion? (0-1)
Scope Scor e — is this claim overgeneralized? "All teenagers" vs "some teenagers" (0-1)
Causal Scor e — is the causal chain actually proven or just implied? (0-1)
The premise with the highest combined vulnerability score gets attacked first.
